Findings overview

The memory footprint of 24/7 recording in places where there isn't a lot of movement can make it problematic to find a specific moment when viewing video data. Therefore, using the correct method and protocol to deal with various situations with video surveillance cameras can facilitate and improve the surveillance work significantly, as well as reduce the recording of unnecessary material.
