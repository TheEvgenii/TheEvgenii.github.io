2D Game C.A.K.E.Z. 2

This game design document describes the details for a 2D run and gun PC game with an original story and characters. The objective of the game is to escape a stinky, toxic, gas-filled laboratory building that was caused by an explosion. We decided to name our game "P.U." based on the common exclamation used by most people when caught in a smelly situation. The term is derived from the latin "Puteo", which means "stink" or "rotten" and is reflected in the gameplay as the stink bomb that infects the laboratory building.

Method and Algorithms for Searching Object in Video Stream with Compression in Video Surveillance System

In this research paper I am working closely with Dr. Shishir and Dr. Mantini on the distributed video systems and optimizations. General solutions for detecting objects in a video stream will be considered. The level of intelligence of video surveillance systems focuses only on moving objects. The new technology should compress video quality as much as possible with a still background image. Thus, it should speed up the process of matching with the database and reduce the need for a large amount of unused memory.
